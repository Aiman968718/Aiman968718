# Final Reflection

Throughout this course, I experienced meaningful growth both academically 
and personally. The Capstone & Professional Practices course helped me 
realize how important it is to combine technical skills with soft skills 
like communication, teamwork, and critical thinking.

One of the key things I learned is that software development is not just 
about writing code, it is about finding solutions to real-world problems 
that affect people's lives. My project on the "Impacts of Social Media on 
Mental Health" gave me a deeper understanding of how technology can shape 
human behavior in both positive and negative ways.

This course also helped me improve my ability to work in an organized 
manner, manage my tasks effectively, and reflect on my own learning 
progress. I became more conscious of the ethical responsibilities that 
come with computing, particularly around user privacy and digital 
well-being.

The most important lesson I took away from this course is the value of 
continuous learning. In the field of Computer Science, technology changes 
very quickly, so it is essential to keep building on existing skills and 
staying updated with new knowledge.

In the future, I plan to apply everything I have learned in real-world 
projects and continue growing my skills in software development, 
Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity.

Overall, this course has been a truly valuable experience that helped me 
develop as a student and has prepared me well for my professional journey 
ahead.
