# Week 7 — Reflection

## Topic Covered: Ethics in Computing (AI Bias & Cybersecurity)

## What I Learned

This week I learned about AI bias and how systems can become unfair 
when they are trained on biased or unrepresentative data. I also 
learned about ethics in cybersecurity, including the importance of 
protecting user data and maintaining confidentiality in all digital 
systems.

## Key Insight

Technology must be both fair and secure in order to avoid causing 
harm to users and communities. Building systems that are biased or 
insecure is not just a technical failure, it is an ethical one.

## Real-Life Application

In my future projects, I will be more careful about how data is used 
and make sure that the systems I build treat all users fairly. I will 
also keep data privacy and security in mind at every stage of the 
development process.
