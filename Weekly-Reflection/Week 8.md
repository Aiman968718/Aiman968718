# Week 8 — Reflection

## Topic Covered: Industry Interaction

## What I Learned

This week I learned how industry collaboration helps students better 
understand real-world problems and professional expectations. Connecting 
with industry professionals also helps in making better decisions when 
choosing projects and career paths.

## Key Insight

Practical exposure is just as important as theoretical knowledge. 
Classroom learning gives us the foundation, but real-world interaction 
helps us understand how to actually apply that knowledge in a 
professional setting.

## Real-Life Application

I plan to actively attend seminars, guest lectures, and networking 
events to gain more industry knowledge. Interacting with professionals 
will help me better prepare for the challenges of a real working 
environment.
