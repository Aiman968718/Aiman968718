# Week 5 — Reflection

## Topic Covered: Codes of Conduct (IEEE, ACM, BCS)

## What I Learned

This week I learned about the ethical guidelines that professionals in 
the computing field are expected to follow, such as honesty, protecting 
user privacy, and treating others fairly. Different professional codes 
like IEEE, ACM, and BCS all highlight the responsibility that developers 
have towards society and the people who use their systems.

## Key Insight

Ethics play a very important role in guiding decision-making, especially 
in difficult or unclear situations. Having a strong ethical foundation 
helps professionals make the right choices even when there is pressure 
to do otherwise.

## Real-Life Application

I will apply these ethical principles in my own work by avoiding 
plagiarism, respecting data privacy, and always being honest in my 
academic and professional activities. These values will guide me 
throughout my career as a software developer.
