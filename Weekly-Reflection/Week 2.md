# Week 2 — Reflection

## Topic Covered: CS in Society (Historical, Social & Economic Impact)

## What I Learned

This week I learned how computing has developed from early machines to 
modern technologies like Artificial Intelligence and cloud computing. 
These changes have greatly affected communication, education, and job 
markets around the world. I also got a better understanding of important 
issues like data privacy and the digital divide, and how they impact 
different communities.

## Key Insight

Technology has a deep effect on society, which means that developers 
carry a real responsibility in how they design and build systems. The 
choices we make as developers can either help or harm the people who use 
our products.

## Real-Life Application

Going forward, I will be more mindful of how the software I develop 
affects users and society as a whole. I want to make sure that the 
systems I build are thoughtful, responsible, and considerate of the 
broader impact they may have on people's lives.
