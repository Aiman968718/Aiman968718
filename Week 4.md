# Week 4 — Reflection

## Topic Covered: Personal Branding

## What I Learned

This week I learned how to build a professional identity using platforms 
like LinkedIn, GitHub, and a personal portfolio. It helped me understand 
that having a strong online presence is very important for career growth 
and making a good impression in the professional world.

## Key Insight

Your online profile speaks for you before you even get the chance to meet 
an employer in person. A well-maintained and professional online presence 
can open doors to many career opportunities.

## Real-Life Application

I applied this learning by creating and updating my LinkedIn and GitHub 
profiles and starting to build my own portfolio. This has already helped 
me become more aware of how I present myself professionally online.
